Caprese Salad with Balsamic Glaze

Description

A refreshing salad showcasing the classic flavors of ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and aromatic basil drizzled with a tangy balsamic glaze. Perfect for a light lunch or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 8 oz (225g) fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Slice tomatoes and fresh mozzarella into rounds. Arrange them alternately on a serving platter.
  2. Layer fresh basil leaves on top of the tomato and mozzarella slices.
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the salad to taste.
  4. Drizzle balsamic glaze over the salad, concentrating on the tomatoes and mozzarella.
  5. Serve immediately as a light and flavorful appetizer or side dish. Enjoy your Caprese Salad with Balsamic Glaze!
